# Sun Tracker

**See where the sun is, right now.**

## The Problem

Photographers need to know exactly where the sun will be for golden hour. Hikers want to know how much daylight is left. Solar panel owners need to understand sun paths. But checking sunrise/sunset times doesn't tell you where to look in the sky right now.

## The Solution

Sun Tracker is a mobile-first PWA that shows the sun's exact position in real-time, overlaid on your phone's camera feed. Point your phone at the sky and see a glowing indicator where the sun actually is. Get live azimuth, elevation, sunrise/sunset times with compass directions, and golden hour countdown.

## Who It's For

- Photographers planning golden hour shots
- Outdoor enthusiasts and hikers
- Solar energy enthusiasts
- Anyone curious about where the sun is

## Key Features

- Live sun position (azimuth + elevation) updated every second
- Camera overlay with glowing sun indicator
- Compass heading with cardinal directions
- Sunrise and sunset times with compass bearings
- Golden hour countdown
- Works offline after first load
- No account needed — pure client-side
- Single HTML file, zero dependencies (except SunCalc CDN)
- Dark astronomical instrument UI aesthetic

## Tech Stack

Pure HTML + CSS + Vanilla JS, SunCalc, Geolocation API, DeviceOrientation API, getUserMedia
